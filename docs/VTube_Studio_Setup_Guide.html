1. What is VTube Studio?

VTube Studio (VTS) is a free app that uses your webcam or smartphone to track your face and animate Live2D Cubism models. It is the industry standard for VTubing.

  • Supports Live2D Cubism models (NOT 3D/VRM)
  • Free on Steam with optional PRO upgrade
  • WebSocket API for plugins and external control
  • Works on Windows, macOS, iOS, Android

2. Download & Install

Desktop (Recommended)

Step 1: Open Steam on your computer

Step 2: Search for "VTube Studio" in the Steam Store

Step 3: Click "Add to Library" (FREE)

Step 4: Click "Install"

Step 5: Wait for download (~200MB)

Direct: store.steampowered.com/app/1325860

3. First Launch

Step 1: Launch VTube Studio from Steam

Step 2: Dismiss welcome popups

Step 3: Grant camera permission

Step 4: Default model (Akari) loads automatically

Step 5: Face tracking starts immediately!

4. Enable API (Port 8001)

Step 1: Click the gear icon (Settings) in main menu

Step 2: Go to "General" tab

Step 3: Find "Plugin API" section

Step 4: Toggle "Start API" to ON

Step 5: Confirm port is 8001 (default)

When Pembantu AI connects, VTube Studio will ask for permission. Click "Allow".

5. Connect to Pembantu AI

Edit your .env file:

VTS_ENABLED=true
VTS_HOST=localhost
VTS_PORT=8001
VTS_AUTO_RECONNECT=true
VTS_RECONNECT_INTERVAL=5.0

Step 1: Run python app.py

Step 2: Open Pembantu AI in browser

Step 3: Click settings icon

Step 4: Toggle "Sambung ke VTube Studio" ON

Step 5: Click "Allow" in VTS dialog

Step 6: Status shows green "Disambung"

6. Setting Up Hotkeys

Step 1: In VTS, go to Settings -> Hotkeys

Step 2: Click "Add Hotkey"

Step 3: Create hotkeys with EXACT IDs:

Hotkey IDDescriptionAssign Animation
wave_helloWave greetingAny wave animation
nod_head_agreeNod agreementAny nod animation
explain_arm_gestureExplainingAny explain animation
explain_hand_leftPoint leftAny point animation
explain_hand_rightPoint rightAny point animation
idle_waitingIdleAny idle animation
IDs must match EXACTLY. Do not change spelling or casing.

7. Mouth Parameter Binding

Step 1: In VTS, go to Settings -> Parameters

Step 2: Add parameter binding:

  • Input: MouthOpen
  • Output: ParamMouthOpenY

Step 3: Map: IN 0-1, OUT 0-1

Step 4: Enable Smoothing

VTS only allows injection to tracking (input) parameters, not Live2D (output) directly. Binding is required.

8. Load a Live2D Model

Step 1: Click "Change VTS Model" in main menu

Step 2: Click "Import your own Model"

Step 3: Navigate to your model folder (.moc3 file)

Step 4: Select folder and click OK

VTS comes with built-in models. Free models available on Booth.pm.

9. Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
"VTS connector not initialized"Set VTS_ENABLED=true, restart
"Connection refused"VTS running, API on port 8001
Mouth not movingBind MouthOpen to ParamMouthOpenY
"Parameter not found"Create binding in VTS Parameters
Tracking choppyMore smoothing, better lighting
Hotkeys not workingCheck IDs match exactly (case-sensitive)
Camera not detectedCheck Windows camera permissions
